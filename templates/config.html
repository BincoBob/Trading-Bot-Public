Strategy Explanation

How the Trading Bot Works

The Bitcoin trading bot uses a moving average strategy to make buy and sell decisions:

Condition Action Explanation
Price > MA and Price ≤ MA + $5 Buy {{ config.buying_percentage * 100 }}% of balance Small upward trend detected, buying opportunity
Price > MA + $5 and Price ≤ MA + $10 Sell {{ config.selling_percentage_moderate * 100 }}% of BTC Moderate price increase, take some profit
Price > MA + $10 Sell {{ config.selling_percentage_high * 100 }}% of BTC Significant price increase, take larger profit
Price > Sell Threshold ({{ config.verkaufswert }}) Sell 25% of BTC Price exceeded predefined threshold
